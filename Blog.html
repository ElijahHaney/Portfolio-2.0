What is your This Week's Interesting Thing for this week?

The most interesting I learned in the daily ticket this week was that GIF's are being replaced with MP4 files. This was a little bit of a shock to me as the MP4 format is not as good as the GIF format is at its job. For example GIF's have a seamless transition in the looping of the images, while MP4's are a bit clunkier and usually have a short pause before it transitions. Despite this fact the GIF's are still slowly being replaced because of how old the technology they use is. For example the file size is really large causing you to have to greatly lower the resolution to lower the file size. For this reason they are being replaced by a bit of a more efficient file format in the form of an MP4.

What is your This Week's Interesting Thing for this week?

This weeks interesting thing is Two factor authentication (TFA). This is a technology that requires you to not only use your password to log in, but also requires you to accept the request on another device like a phone or a fob. This is great for safety as if someone wants to hack you they need to access your password and the other device used for authentication.
